ABOUT

Hello!

My name is Kalib Hubbard, I use he/him/his pronouns, I’m 20 years old, and I am a bicycle mechanic and graphic designer based out of Richmond VA. I am currently working on getting my bachelors degree in graphic design from Virginia Commonwealth University. I have been involved with cycling since I started cross country mountain bike racing at 14 and have been working as a mechanic since I was 17.

I was born and raised in Athens Georgia, a small town not too far from Atlanta, known for its arts and music scene, as well as being the home of The University of Georgia. I was raised by two very artistic parents, surrounded by countless other artists and creatives which I believe helped me kindle my passion for the arts. I have always loved creating but it wasn’t til my sophomore year of highschool that I discovered my affinity for digital art and graphic design.

Thanks for checking out my website!

Feel free to reach out to me via the contact form or by email at kalibhubbardart@gmail.com
